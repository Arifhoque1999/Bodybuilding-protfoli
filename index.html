Bodybuilding for Women: Overcoming Stereotypes and Embracing Strength

Bodybuilding is a form of exercise and physical training that focuses on building and sculpting the muscles of the body through the use of weightlifting and other resistance exercises. It is often performed with the goal of improving muscle size, shape, and symmetry, as well as increasing strength and endurance. Bodybuilding programs typically involve a combination of weightlifting exercises that target specific muscle groups, as well as cardiovascular exercise and a healthy diet to support muscle growth and improve overall physical conditioning. Bodybuilding competitions, also known as physique contests, are events in which competitors are judged on the size, symmetry, and overall appearance of their muscles.
